Ghostable LLC (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Ghostable platform (“Service”). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account Info:** When you sign up, you provide your name and email—used to personalize your account and send essential communications.
- **Support Requests:** If you contact us with questions or support requests, we retain transcripts and your email for reference.
- **Usage Data:** We collect anonymized data (e.g., page visits, feature usage) to improve our Service performance and usability.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- To operate and improve Ghostable.
- To respond to support inquiries and provide assistance.
- To send service notifications or important updates.
- We never sell your personal data or share it for marketing without your explicit consent.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We only share your personal information:
- With third parties to provide the Service (e.g., Stripe, AWS, email services).
- When legally required (e.g., valid court order). We’ll notify you unless prohibited.
- In the event of a merger or acquisition—only after notifying you.

---

## 4. Data Security

- All communications are encrypted via SSL/TLS.
- Sensitive data and backups are encrypted at rest using AES‑256 encryption.
- We log access to accounts by IP and user for security audits.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

- Account data is retained while your account is active.
- After account cancellation, data becomes inaccessible immediately and is **permanently deleted within 30 days**, including backups.
- You may request account or data deletion at any time by contacting support.

---

## 6. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to understand feature usage and website performance. Data is anonymized and used solely to improve our Services.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at **support@ghostable.dev**. We will respond promptly.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be notified via email or through our Service. Continued use signifies acceptance.

---

Thank you for trusting Ghostable—we’re committed to keeping your data safe and transparent.
